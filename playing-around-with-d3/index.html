Playing around with D3

February 13, 2016

During my holiday week I played around with D3 js. I was fascinated by D3 since the first time I saw it, but I never had time to really play with it. Now I did - a little bit…

My sample data was the number of kilometers I drove with my Vespa in Đà Nẵng over time. I tracked this data by making a screen shot of my km counter from time to time. So I just flipped thru my photo gallery and looked for pictures of my tachometer. I entered the data in a plain text file. The I followed some tutorials and tinkered around until I got a curve the way I wanted it.
